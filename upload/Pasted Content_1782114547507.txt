Section 1: Company Overview

Chittety Construction is a construction supply, sourcing, vendor network, wholesale distribution, and project support company serving residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure-related requirements. The company is built on more than a decade of practical industry experience, direct vendor relationships, and ground-level understanding of how construction projects actually move from planning to material sourcing, delivery, installation, and maintenance.

Unlike a standard contractor listing website or a simple service-booking platform, Chittety Construction operates as a trusted bridge between customers, contractors, vendors, suppliers, and service providers. The company helps clients identify the right materials, compare available options, request quotations, source products through vendor networks, and coordinate service support where required.

The business supports a wide range of construction categories including plumbing, sewer line materials, pumps and water systems, electrical wiring, lighting, flooring, tiles, roofing, waterproofing, steel fabrication, building materials, door hardware, modular kitchen accessories, tools, safety equipment, and project-based procurement requirements.

Section 2: Our Story

Chittety Construction was created with a simple but important purpose: to make construction material sourcing more dependable, transparent, and accessible for homeowners, contractors, builders, businesses, and project teams.

Over the years, the company has worked closely with vendors and construction professionals at ground level. This hands-on experience has helped Chittety Construction understand the real challenges customers face while sourcing materials: inconsistent pricing, unclear product specifications, limited vendor availability, delays in supply, difficulty comparing alternatives, and lack of reliable coordination between product supply and service execution.

Through consistent field experience and vendor interaction, Chittety Construction developed a strong understanding of construction supply chains and built a wide network of material providers, contractors, technicians, distributors, and specialist vendors. This network allows the company to support both small and large requirements, from single product inquiries to multi-category procurement needs for commercial and residential projects.
